Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026

Aiden He ("we", "our", or "us") operates Pot Trainer (the "App"), a pot-limit poker training application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a Firebase Authentication user ID. You may also sign in using Google Sign-In, which provides us with your basic profile information (name and email).

b) Guest Mode

If you use the App without creating an account, your training progress (questions answered, ads watched, unlocked games) is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to our servers.

c) Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display rewarded advertisements in guest mode. AdMob may collect your advertising ID, IP address, country/region, and usage/interaction data. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

d) Locally Stored Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Authentication tokens (for logged-in users)
  • Guest mode progress
  • Table layout configuration and preferences
  • App settings (volume, language)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the App's training functionality
  • Authenticate your account and manage access
  • Display rewarded ads in guest mode
  • Save your training progress, layout preferences, and settings

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — for user account management
  • Google Sign-In — for optional social login
  • Google AdMob — for displaying rewarded advertisements
  • Cloud Firestore — for storing service permissions

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Your data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide the App's functionality.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. Locally stored data (training progress, layout configuration, settings) can be removed by uninstalling the App or clearing app data in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to remove that information promptly.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device, and all communications with Firebase services are encrypted via HTTPS.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Use the App in guest mode without providing personal information

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
